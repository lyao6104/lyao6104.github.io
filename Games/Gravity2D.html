2D Gravity Sandbox

Instructions

Watch dust specks be drawn to one another by the force of gravity. If speck collision is on, they will tend to coalesce together into one entity.

Note: Graphics quality has been reduced in this web version for performance reasons.

Controls: Click or Tap to add dust specks, up to a maximum of 200. Press Spacebar to toggle speck collision.
